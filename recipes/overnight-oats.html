Overnights Oats Recipe

Description

Overnight oats are a tasty, nutrious and healthy breakfast. Oats swore an oath that they will behave at night and develop into a tasty mushy mass. They are yet to dissapoint.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Yogurt
  • Oats
  • Honey
  • Chia seeds
  • Cinnamon
  • Berries

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a jar, seal, and shake. Important use the oxford comma.
  2. Open the jar and fold in the fruit.
  3. Seal the jar and refigerate overnight.
