Pho Recipe

Description

Pho invented by the Phoenicians when they first arrived in Vietnom. Vietnom later changed their name to Vietnam despit all the delicious food.

Ingredients

  • Beef bones and tough beef cuts
  • Flat rice noodles
  • Aromatics
  • Spices
  • Daikon
  • Salt, sugar and fish sauce
  • Steak

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Make broth.
  3. Preheat oven.
  4. Roast onion and ginger.
  5. Strain broth and discard excessive fat.
  6. Cook rice noodles.
  7. Thinly slice meat.
  8. Serve and enjoy.
